\MonsterStatsDescription{
    The statistics presented here describe nocturnal birds of prey from 30 to 60 centimeters long, with wingspans up to 1.80 meter. They combine both talons into a single attack.
}

Owls swoop quietly down onto prey, attacking with their powerful talons.

\textbf{Skills:} Owls have a +8 racial bonus on \skill{Listen} checks and a +14 racial bonus on \skill{Move Silently} checks. *They have a +8 racial bonus on \skill{Spot} checks in areas of shadowy illumination.
