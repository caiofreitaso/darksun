\MonsterStatsDescription{
    These birds of prey inhabit nearly every terrain and climate, though they all prefer high, secluded nesting spots.

    A typical eagle is about 1 meter long and has a wingspan of about 2 meters. The statistics presented here can describe any similar-sized, diurnal bird of prey.
}

Eagles dive at prey, raking with their powerful talons.

\textbf{Skills:} Eagles have a +8 racial bonus on \skill{Spot} checks.
