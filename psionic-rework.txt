PSIONIC REWORK

-> Must be successful in a SPECIAL ABILITY CHECK (use modifier * 1.5, DC 9 + power level).
	-> 1 is always failure. Spend power points without effects.
	-> 20 is always success. Spend half of the power points.
	-> May expend psionic focus to take 10 at the test
-> 3 successful psionic attacks allow use of other mind-affecting powers.

EGO WHIP
Telepathy [Mind-Affecting]
Level:            Psion/wilder 1
Display:          Auditory
Manifesting Time: 1 standard action
Range:            Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Target:           One creature
Duration:         1 round; see text
Saving Throw:     Will negates
Power Resistance: Yes
Power Points:     1

Dazed.
IF CONTACT: Sickened for 1d4 rounds.

Augment:
	+2 pp: Increase range to Medium (2nd level)
	+4 pp: Increase range to Long (3rd level)
