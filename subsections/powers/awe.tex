\Psionic{Awe}{awe}
{Telepathy (Compulsion) [Fear, Mind-Affecting]}
{
	\textbf{Level:}
	Telepath 3\\
	\textbf{Manifesting Time:}
	1 mental action\\
	\textbf{Range:}
	18  meters\\
	\textbf{Target:}
	All \emph{contacted} creatures\\
	\textbf{Saving Throw:}
	Will negates\\
	\textbf{Cost:}
	\psionic{contact}\\
	\textbf{Maintenance Cost:}
	3 pp/round\\
	\textbf{Prerequisites:}
	\psionic{contact}\\
	% \textbf{Critical Success:}
	% No saving throw allowed.\\
	% \textbf{Critical Failure:}
	% All \emph{contacted} characters view you as pathetic and ridiculous.\\
}
{
	You make others hold you in awe. Each \emph{contacted} character are frightened by you, unless they succeed on a Will save. They won't attack you unless forced to do so.
}
