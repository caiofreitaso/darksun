\Psionic{Invisibility, Psionic}{psionic invisibility}
{Telepathy (Figment)}
{
    \textbf{Level:}
    Telepath 2\\
    \textbf{Manifesting Time:}
    1 mental action\\
    \textbf{Range:}
    90 meters\\
    \textbf{Target:}
    One \emph{contacted} creature\\
    \textbf{Saving Throw:}
    Will disbelief (if interacted with)\\
    \textbf{Cost:}
    \psionic{contact}\\
    \textbf{Maintenance Cost:}
    2 pp/minute\\
    \textbf{Prerequisites:}
    \psionic{mindlink}\\
}

    As the \spell{invisibility} spell, except as noted here.

    You are invisible only in the mind of the target creature.
